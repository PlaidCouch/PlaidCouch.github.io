Welcome to the Plaid Couch Radio website.

Now, you might be asking yourself: "What is Plaid Couch Radio?"
Do not worry! This little ditty below should answer your question.

Basically, we're a bunch of people who decided it would be sick to do a radio show together.
We play whatever we want, but we'll try not to assault your ears too much.
Our main genres are: Rock, Rap, Indie, Alternative, R&B, and old throwback songs.

Our show runs from 5pm to 6pm on Tuesdays, every week.
Feel free to tune in, give us a call, or request a song. Anything really.
