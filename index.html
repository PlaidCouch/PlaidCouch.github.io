Welcome to the Scoot Or Die website.

Now, you might be asking yourself: "What is the Scoot Or Die show?"
Don't worry. We'll explain.

Basically, I'm just a dude who plays music. That's it pretty much.
I mainly play: Rock, Indie, Alternative, R&B, and old throwback songs. To be honest though, I really just play whatever I feel like playing that day.

Our show runs from 4pm to 5pm on Tuesdays, every week.
Feel free to tune in, give me a call, or request a song. Anything really.
